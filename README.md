README:
पथVique 
Your city. Live. Understood.
पथVique is a real-time urban infrastructure platform that bridges the gap between city governments and the people who actually live in them. Not another map app. Not another chatbot wrapper. A full digital twin of urban life — construction zones, flood risk, blocked roads, citizen reports — all in one place, moving in real time.

The Problem
Urban infrastructure is publicly funded but privately experienced. A road gets blocked, a zone floods, a footpath disappears under scaffolding — and the only people who know are the ones already stuck there.
Meanwhile, the government has no reliable signal from the ground, and citizens have no reliable channel to the system. The data exists. The cameras exist. The complaints exist. None of it connects.
पथVique is the missing layer between a city and its people — where citizen experience becomes city intelligence, and city intelligence becomes personal decisions.

What It Does (Utilizing Data to the Fullest) :
Smart Chatbot — Ask it anything about your city's infrastructure. "Is the main road near Velachery flooded?" It knows. Real answers, not redirects.
Smart Routing — Routes that actually update. Construction on your usual road? Waterlogging alert in that zone? पथVique filters it out before you even start driving. Integrated with Google Maps but smarter about local conditions.
Citizen Reporting — Spot a pothole, a broken streetlight, a flooded road? Upload a photo. It gets tagged, logged, and sent straight to the government dashboard. No forms. No follow-up emails into the void.
Disaster Score — Every zone in the city gets a live risk score. Flood-prone areas, construction-heavy corridors, accident blackspots — visualised on the map so you can make informed decisions before you leave home.
Government Dashboard — The other side of the same system. Authorities see everything citizens report, mapped and categorised. Analytics on which areas need attention. A direct line to the people they serve.

Tech Stack

Frontend: Handled by React, which renders the UI and communicates with the Backend.
Backend: Powered by Node.js with Express, managing data flow, business logic, and API requests.
AI / Data: Processes are executed in Python, handling data analysis and machine learning tasks.
Maps: Leverages the Google Maps API for geolocation, routing, and map visualizations.
Database: Utilizing Firebase, which provides real-time data storage, authentication, and hosting services.
Image Uploads: Managed by Cloudinary, a robust solution for uploading, transforming, and delivering images.

Why पथVique
Cities collect data constantly. Cameras, sensors, service requests, weather feeds. Almost none of it reaches the person deciding whether to take the highway or the back road this morning.
पथVique makes that data useful, personal, and two-directional. Citizens inform the city. The city informs citizens. Infrastructure stops being something that just happens to people.

Impact Potential :


Over 500 million urban Indians commute daily with no real-time access to ground-level infrastructure data — they rely on outdated maps, word of mouth, or discovering blockages after they're already in them.
Municipal corporations across India receive thousands of civic complaints weekly, yet the average resolution time exceeds 7 days — largely because there is no structured, location-tagged pipeline from citizens to the right government department.
Cities already have the infrastructure — cameras, sensors, weather feeds, service records — but none of it is connected into a single actionable layer. PathVique doesn't require new hardware. It makes existing city data finally useful to the people paying for it.
